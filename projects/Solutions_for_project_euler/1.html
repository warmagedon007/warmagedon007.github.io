Problem 1:

If we list all the natural numbers below 10 that are multiples of 3 or 5, we get 3, 5, 6 and 9. The sum of these multiples is 23. Find the sum of all the multiples of 3 or 5 below 1000.

Explanation:

lets start small, lets sum all the multiples of 2 or 3 under 10, so we have, 2, 4, 6, 8 as multiples of 10. and 3, 6, 9 as multiples of 3. as you can see, we count 6 twice. On a larger scale of the problem. we need to remove duplicates. so lets count the steps:

  1. Iterate and sum the multiples of 3
  2. Iterate on the multiples of 5, check if they are devided by 3, if they are, dont sum them.
